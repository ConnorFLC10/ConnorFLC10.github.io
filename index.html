The Overall Design

This design is what we would ideally have for our users. Just a simple and comfy mat that goes nicely onto your chair. It also would have the main circuitry in a casing which is securely stuck underneath the chair.

The Circuit Design

The power supply uses a 5V regulator. The circuit consist of resistive sensors and a voltage divider in order for the device to tell if the user's seating position needs to be adjusted. And the given speaker is setup to output the audio cues when

The Product Casing

The casing is a 3D printed cuboid that contains all the main circuitry. This would be secured to the bottom of the user's chair. And the resistive sensors would protrude and be embedded safely in an ideally comfortable yoga mat sort of material that would be placed onto the user's chair.

We're here to make sure your day goes by just spine!

Is this product really necessary?

Back pain is the number one reason for workers missing work in United States. This product is aimed at those that spend a majority of their time on a chair infront of a computer. This includes office workers and students. An overwhelming majority of office workers will develop chronic back pain sometime in their life and is always a growing issue all across the world as people are sitting infront of a computer screen for longer periods of time. In this time, it is easy to get distracted and forget about your posture which leads to long term issues.
